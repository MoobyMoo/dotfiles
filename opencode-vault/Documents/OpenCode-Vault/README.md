# OpenCode Vault

This vault is no longer a generic session log. It is the durable knowledge and planning layer for your personal AI system.

## Structure

- `00_System/`: operating rules and vault guidance
- `01_Dashboard/`: daily and weekly control pages
- `02_Courses/`: durable course pages
- `03_Capture/`: temporary inbox and fast capture
- `04_Reviews/`: weekly and future review notes
- `05_Templates/`: reusable note templates
- `99_Archive/legacy/`: old generic scaffolding kept only for reference

## Mission fit

This vault should help the assistant:

- understand your student life in a durable way
- reduce scattered information
- surface deadlines and next actions clearly
- improve over time instead of restarting from scraps

## Rule of thumb

- `Google Keep` = fast messy capture
- `Google Tasks` = execution layer
- `Google Calendar` = hard commitments and due dates
- `OpenCode Vault` = durable structure and knowledge
